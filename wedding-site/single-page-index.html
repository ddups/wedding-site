Friday

4 pm - on
Open check-in -- Grab your bag from the main office and find your cabin.
7 pm - 9 pm
Join us in the Rec Center for post-dinner drinks, dancing, and mingling!
9 pm - on
Grab a spot by the fire pit, kick back, and relax

Saturday

9:30 am - 11 am
Brunch at the rec hall. Have some breakfast and experiment at the mimosa bar!
11 am - 12 pm
Pick-up Ultimate game in front of the boy's quad. Everyone is encouraged to come play or watch.
12 pm - 2 pm
Open camp!! Go canoeing, play some disc golf or any of the other games and activities.
4:45 pm - 5:45 pm
Cocktail hour on the Rec Hall lawn. Apps, drinks, and a chance to chat with the newly-weds.
4 pm - 4:45 pm
WE GET MARRIED!! Make sure to bring your sunglasses, cameras, and some tissues!
2 pm - 3:30 pm
Time to get ready and head to the Rec Hall lawn for some pre-freshments, then hit the beach.
5:45 pm - 7:30 pm
Introductions, first dance, speeches, and the main reason you came, DINNER!
7:30 pm - 10:30 pm
Time to put on those dancing shoes, grab a partner, and groove the night away.
10:30 pm - on
Come to a bon fire in the woods or feel free to keep the party rocking all night!

Sunday

9:30 am - 10:30 am
Cure your hangover with bacon and eggs! And a little hair of the dog, of course...
10:30 am - 12 pm
Break down, clean up, and check out. Hopefully you will be leaving with some great memories, and some your dignity intact!
